I’m a designer and developer living in Stockholm. In the daytime, I work on websites and services at the digital agency Divide & Conquer. In the evenings, I tinker with my own projects, like my free WordPress themes.

I enjoy designing and building things that look nice and work well, whether it’s websites, services or brands. I’ve been fortunate enough to have that be my day job, at the digital agency Divide & Conquer located in Vasastan, Stockholm.

At Divide & Conquer, I have the privilege of working for fun clients together with some of my favorite people: Thord D. Hedengren, Helena Waern and Emma Fresk. We do good work, mostly with WordPress. If you need a hand with your next project, you should get in touch. We work with both local and international clients.

In addition to client work, I tend to have a couple of side projects cooking. Most recently, I launched WP Glossary. I also write about Nintendo on the Swedish site Kong. The side project I’m best known for is my collection of free WordPress themes. If you have questions about them, make sure you check out the FAQ before you email me.
